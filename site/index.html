The Wedding

Due to the health and safety of our families and friends, Kevin and Andrea have made the decision to postpone their wedding date to July 2, 2021. Here are some important details:

The wedding will take place on July 3, 2020 at Padua Hills Theater in Claremont, CA.

Guests may arrive at 6:00PM and the ceremony will take place at exactly 6:30PM. Following the cocktail hour, reception will begin at approximately 7:00PM and will go until 11:30PM.

THERE WILL BE ABSOLUTELY NO PHONES ALLOWED DURING THE CEREMONY! We spent a lot of money hiring a photographer, so please allow him to take professional pictures! There is nothing more frustrating for a photographer than seeing a phone screen blocking the couple. You will of course be allowed to take photos and video during the reception, but if your phone is out during the ceremony, one of the staff members will kindly ask you to turn it off and put it away.

All photography and videography is shot by Keeyahtay Lewis, a long time friend of Andrea’s and extremely talented photographer.

Though our reception will have activities for friends and family of all ages, there will be both alcohol and cannabis bars for our guests that are over 21. The venue has stressed that if they find any underage drinkers and/or smokers, the entire reception will be called off. The venue also has the names and faces of those who are not of legal drinking or smoking age. We know you kiddos just want to join in on the fun, but the law is the law! Please do not ruin it for everyone.

Worried about what you’re going to wear? No problem! Dress attire is formal, so please no jeans or sneakers. Andrea also requests that you do NOT wear white (she is a gemini and exudes Big Gemini Energy… please don’t make her upset...) Our wedding color is lavender; purple looks great on everyone!

Travel Information

If you're flying from out of town, we suggest flying into LAX and renting a car. The venue is about 50 miles West.

Ontario Airport is much closer to the venue, but can be less cost-effective. However, it is up to you to do your own research! Keep in mind if you are flying to and from LAX, you will have to either rent a car or take a cab to your hotel destination.

There will be a block of discounted rooms reserved at the DoubleTree Claremont for $139 a night. Please call (909) 626-2411 to book your room; use the code "GDW". We encourage guests to book early!

There will be a shuttle bus between the DoubleTree and the venue at convenient times. (Schedule to come.)

Uninterested in staying at a hotel? There are multiple beautiful AirBnB’s in Claremont! Claremont is a few miles away from the Angeles National Forest… there’s nothing more American than seeing a beautiful National Forest on the 4th of July!

As previously stated, the venue is about an hour west of Los Angeles. If you are interested in staying in Los Angeles, we recommend staying in Culver City, West Hollywood, or Venice Beach.
